PARC: Contrast

Amazon

Amazon

On the Amazon homepage, diverse themes of contrast are evident in the design. The use of contrasting fonts is notable, serving to establish hierarchy and emphasize more important text. Red signs strategically signal discounts, creating a visual contrast with the plain black price tags. Additionally, yellow boxed text is employed to highlight and signify the locations of buttons to press on the webpage, ensuring clarity and guiding user interaction. These deliberate contrasts contribute to an organized and visually engaging user experience on the Amazon homepage.
